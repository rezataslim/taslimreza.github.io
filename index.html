About Me

Prof. Dr. Md. Taslim Reza is an academician, researcher, and engineering consultant with extensive experience in higher education, research, and academic administration. He joined the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) at Uttara University on 5 May 2026 as a Professor. Prior to this appointment, he served as Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Islamic University of Technology, a subsidiary organ of the OIC, where he contributed for more than a decade in teaching, research, laboratory development, and institutional management. He is also serving as Senior Consultant at Imperial Engineering and Consultancy and has been actively involved in the development of engineering laboratories and academic programs in different universities. Prof. Reza completed his Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from IUT with distinction and OIC scholarship. He earned his Master’s degree in Biomedical Engineering from Tampere University of Technology, Finland, where he secured first position and received the prestigious Rector’s Award for outstanding academic performance. His master’s research on digital image processing was fully funded by Nokia. He later obtained his PhD from IUT with research focused on breast elastography modulus reconstruction and medical imaging techniques. His research interests include medical image processing, biomedical instrumentation, digital signal processing, elastography imaging, machine learning applications in healthcare, and optical communication. He has published numerous articles in reputed international journals and conferences, and his research contributions have received international recognition, including the Best Paper Award at the International Conference on Machine Vision and Applications (ICMVA 2023), Singapore. In addition to academic and research activities, Prof. Reza has extensive administrative experience. At IUT, he served as Head of Planning, Procurement and Development, Assistant Provost of the Male Hall, Laboratory In-Charge of the Biomedical Laboratory, and member of several institutional committees. He has also been actively involved in laboratory establishment, curriculum development, student affairs, and academic quality assurance. Prof. Reza is a member of Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers and The Institution of Engineers, Bangladesh. His dedication to academic excellence, research innovation, and institutional development continues to contribute significantly to engineering education and research in Bangladesh.

Research Interests

Medical Image Processing

Development of image processing algorithms for biomedical imaging, including ultrasound image analysis, artifact reduction, lesion segmentation, and medical image enhancement.

Biomedical Signal Processing

Application of signal processing techniques to biomedical signals such as EEG, ECG, EMG, and physiological recordings for diagnosis, monitoring, and assistive technologies.

Medical Instrumentation

Design and development of biomedical measurement systems, low-cost medical devices, diagnostic tools, and sensor-based healthcare technologies.

Digital Signal Processing

Algorithm development and signal analysis for communication systems, biomedical applications, image processing, and system performance improvement.
